# 3-Minute Pitch Script + Demo Flow

## Duration Plan

- 0:00-0:30 Problem
- 0:30-1:00 Solution
- 1:00-2:30 Live Demo
- 2:30-3:00 Impact + Closing

## Script

### 0:00-0:30 Problem

"India me majority users ke paas structured financial plan nahi hai. Human advisors expensive hain, usually high net-worth clients ko serve karte hain. Is gap ko solve karne ke liye humne FinGuru AI banaya hai."

### 0:30-1:00 Solution

"FinGuru AI ek multi-agent personal money mentor hai jo 4 core outputs deta hai: Money Health Score, FIRE SIP Planner, Tax Optimizer, aur AI Advisor Chat. Iska goal hai confused saver ko confident investor banana."

### 1:00-2:30 Demo Walkthrough

1. Onboarding JSON/Profile input open karein.
2. Click `Run Financial Plan`.
3. Show Money Health Score + 6 dimensions.
4. Show FIRE output:
- monthly SIP
- goal-wise plan
- emergency target
- insurance gap
5. Tax Optimizer run karein:
- old vs new regime
- tax saved value
6. AI Chat ask karein in English.
7. AI Chat V2 run karein in Hindi + voice-enabled toggle.

### 2:30-3:00 Impact + Closing

"Agar 1,000 users annually use karte hain aur average INR 5,000 tax improve hota hai, to INR 50 lakh direct tax impact banta hai. Advisory fee avoidance aur time recovery include karein to estimated annual impact INR 2.8 crore tak ja sakta hai. FinGuru AI personal finance ko truly accessible banata hai."
