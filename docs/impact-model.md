# FinGuru AI Impact Model

## Problem Baseline
- Financial advisors in India can cost INR 25,000+ per year.
- Most salaried users do not have a structured monthly plan for goals, tax, and retirement.

## Assumptions
- Active users in year 1: 1,000
- Average annual tax improvement per user: INR 5,000
- Average annual fee avoidance per user (vs human advisor): INR 20,000
- Time saved per user: 10 hours/year
- Imputed hourly value of user time: INR 300/hour

## Back-of-Envelope Impact

1. Direct tax savings
- 1,000 x 5,000 = INR 50,00,000

2. Advisor cost avoided
- 1,000 x 20,000 = INR 2,00,00,000

3. Time value recovered
- 1,000 x 10 x 300 = INR 30,00,000

## Total Estimated Economic Impact
- INR 2,80,00,000 per 1,000 active users annually

## Strategic Business Lens
- Lower acquisition friction due to free onboarding score.
- Higher retention due to recurring monthly plan updates and AI chat utility.
- Potential monetization:
  - Freemium score + paid advanced planning
  - B2B2C with payroll/fintech partners
